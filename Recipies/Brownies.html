Choclate brownies

A quick and easy desert that will have you cheating on your diet as you copme back for more and more.

Ingredients

  • 1 box pillsbury milk choclate brownie mix
  • 2/3 cupoil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2eggs
  • non stick cooking spray or butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Greese Pan
  3. Grab a bowl and combine all ingredients and mix until blended
  4. Pour into pan and cook for 28-30 minutes for a 13x9 inch pan and 30-35 minutes for a 9x9 inch pan
  5. once done baking let the brownies completley cool before cutting and serving
